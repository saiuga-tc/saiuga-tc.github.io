    About Us

    The University of Georgia is located in the music-rich city of Athens, Georgia. Sigma Alpha Iota (SAI) is an international music fraternity. Music is our main focus; whether it is in composition, education, performance, or appreciation, SAI has it all! SAI harbors a strong interest in our members' abilities to succeed in their musical endeavors, career development, and in giving back to their communities.

    The Iota Zeta chapter at UGA is in Region VI and Province Tau A. The Iota Zeta chapter at UGA currently has 84 collegiate members who encompass a variety of majors, career paths, and musical foci. Iota Zeta is part of the Tau A province, which includes all SAI chapters contained within the northern half of the state of Georgia.
